The Man Behind the Apron

A Chef Who Lives
to Tell the Story

Desh Fernando is a Vancouver Island-based chef, photographer, and storyteller whose passion for food goes far beyond the plate. Inspired by adventure and driven by curiosity, Desh has built a culinary world that spans teaching sourdough in condos, capturing breathtaking food photography, and hosting a wild adventure cooking show.

What started with a single sourdough loaf has grown into a multi-faceted creative career — one that connects people with the joy of cooking, the beauty of food, and the thrill of exploration.
